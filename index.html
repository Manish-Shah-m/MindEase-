Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with MindEase

How does online therapy work?

Online therapy through MindEase works just like traditional therapy, but from the comfort of your own space. You'll be matched with a licensed therapist, and you can communicate through secure messaging, video sessions, phone calls, or live chat. Your therapist will provide professional guidance and support tailored to your needs.

Are the therapists licensed and qualified?

Yes, absolutely. All therapists on MindEase are licensed, trained, experienced, and accredited psychologists, marriage and family therapists, clinical social workers, or licensed professional counselors. They have been qualified and certified by their state professional board and have at least 3 years and 2,000 hours of hands-on experience.

Is my information kept private and confidential?

Your privacy is our top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and are HIPAA compliant. All communication between you and your therapist is completely confidential. We will never share your personal information with anyone without your explicit consent, except in rare cases required by law.

Can I switch therapists if needed?

Yes, you can switch therapists at any time for any reason at no additional cost. We want you to find the right fit, and sometimes that takes trying out different therapists. Simply let us know through the app, and we'll match you with a new therapist quickly.

Does insurance cover MindEase?

While MindEase doesn't directly bill insurance companies, many users are able to get reimbursed by their insurance. We provide you with all the necessary documentation to submit to your insurance provider. Additionally, you may be able to use your HSA or FSA to pay for services.

How quickly can I get started?

You can get started today. After signing up and completing our brief questionnaire, you'll typically be matched with a therapist within 24-48 hours. You can begin messaging your therapist immediately after being matched, and schedule your first live session at your convenience.

What if I need help between sessions?

With MindEase, you have unlimited messaging access to your therapist between sessions. While they may not respond immediately, they typically reply within a day. For crisis situations, we recommend calling 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or your local emergency services.
